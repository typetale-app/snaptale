Where to look for code?

If you're exploring the repository, here are the key entry points:

  • src/snaptale.tsx - The entry point that mounts the editor for UMD/Widgets (this exposes the `window.pintura` API).
  • src/editor/page.tsx - The main React wrapper rendering the editor.
  • src/editor/ImageEditor.tsx - The layout component combining the canvas and toolbars.
  • src/editor/context/EditorContext.tsx - The central state management for the entire editor.
