# Product

## Register

product

## Users
- **Developers**: Integrating a lightweight, high-performance image editor into their apps/widgets (as a Pintura replacement). They need clean APIs, small bundle sizes, and simple integration paths.
- **Content Creators & Bloggers**: Who need to perform quick, high-quality image editing (cropping, filters, text overlays, adding stickers/shapes) without the complexity of desktop editor software.

## Product Purpose
To provide a modern, high-performance web-based interactive image editor that runs standalone or embedded. Success looks like an editor that loads instantly, performs canvas operations with zero lag, and allows users to modify and export images with minimal friction.

## Brand Personality
- **Sleek, Playful, Precise**
- Blends high-performance utility and clean, Radix-based minimalism with a creative, vibrant, and interactive spirit (playful stickers, responsive canvas).

## Anti-references
- **Photoshop / GIMP Clutter**: Overly complex, deep menus, and intimidating toolbars.
- **Laggy Rendering**: Delayed frame updates, stuttering preview renders, and slow export times.
- **Sterile Utilities**: Dull, grey, characterless tools that feel like default browser UI rather than a crafted experience.

## Design Principles
- **Instant Response**: Keep all canvas edits (drawings, filter adjustments, cropping) responsive and real-time.
- **Clean Discretion**: Present controls contextual to the active tool only, leaving maximum screen real-estate for the canvas.
- **Controlled Vibrancy**: Keep the UI sleek and professional, but let creative choices (stickers, shapes, fonts) be colorful, playful, and fun.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Target WCAG 2.1 AA guidelines.
- Ensure high contrast for control text (>= 4.5:1).
- Provide clear typography and layout hierarchy.
